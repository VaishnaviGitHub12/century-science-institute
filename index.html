    Our Course

    Career In Engineering

    JEE is an engineering entrance examination conducted for admission to various engineering colleges in India. It is constituted by two different examinations: JEE - Main and the JEE Advanced. The exams are of the objective pattern. JEE Advanced is regarded internationally as one of the most challenging undergraduate admission tests. The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducts the joint admission process.

    Career In Medical

    The National Eligibility Cum Entrance Test-Under Graduate (NEET), succeeded from All India Pre-Medical Test (AIPMT), is an entrance examination in India for students who wish to study undergraduate medical courses (MBBS) and dental courses (BDS) in government or private medical colleges and dental colleges in India. The National Testing Agency (NTA) conducts the joint admission process.

    MHT-CET

    The Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (MHT-CET) - your gateway to higher education in the state of Maharashtra! MHT-CET is an essential examination conducted annually for students aspiring to pursue undergraduate courses in various fields such as engineering, pharmacy, agriculture, and technology in the state's esteemed institutions. The Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell administers MHT-CET, and it serves as a key platform for thousands of young minds to showcase their academic prowess and secure admission to renowned colleges and universities across Maharashtra.
